# Platform Limits & Specs

## X (Twitter)
- **Text:** 280 characters (free), 25,000 (Premium+)
- **Images:** Up to 4 per post, max 5MB each (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WEBP)
- **Video:** Up to 140s, max 512MB
- **Rate limit:** 300 posts/3h (app-level), 200 posts/15min (user-level)
- **Threads:** Chain replies for longer content

## LinkedIn
- **Text:** 3,000 characters (posts), 120,000 (articles)
- **Images:** Up to 9, max 10MB each
- **Video:** Up to 10 min, max 5GB
- **Documents:** PDF/PPT carousel, up to 300 pages
- **Rate limit:** 150 API calls/day per member
- **Best practices:** Use line breaks for readability, first 2 lines visible in feed

## Instagram
- **Caption:** 2,200 characters
- **Hashtags:** Max 30 per post (recommend 5-10)
- **Images:** Square (1:1), Portrait (4:5), Landscape (1.91:1), max 8MB
- **Video (Reels):** Up to 90s, 9:16 aspect ratio
- **Carousel:** Up to 10 images/videos
- **Rate limit:** 25 API publishes/24h
- **Requires media:** Cannot post text-only
