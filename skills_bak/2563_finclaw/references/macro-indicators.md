## Interpreting Key Metrics

### Yield Curve (T10Y2Y)
- **Positive**: Normal economy
- **Near zero/negative**: Recession signal (inverted yield curve)

### VIX
- **<15**: Low volatility, complacent market
- **15-25**: Normal
- **25-35**: Elevated fear
- **>35**: Extreme fear / market stress

### Fed Funds Rate
- **Rising**: Tightening policy (bearish for stocks)
- **Falling**: Easing policy (bullish for stocks)
