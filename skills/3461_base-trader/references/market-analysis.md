# Market Analysis

## Reading Market Conditions

### Bull Market Signs
- ETH making higher highs
- Increasing trading volumes
- New ATHs across sectors
- Retail interest rising
- Positive funding rates

**Strategy**: More aggressive, larger positions, longer holds

### Bear Market Signs
- ETH making lower lows
- Decreasing volumes
- Fear in social sentiment
- Negative funding rates
- Stablecoin dominance rising

**Strategy**: Conservative, smaller positions, faster exits

### Sideways/Ranging
- Price bouncing between levels
- Low volatility
- Decreasing volume
- Mixed sentiment

**Strategy**: Range trade or wait for breakout

## Sector Rotation

### Hot Sectors (rotate capital to strength)
Monitor which sectors are leading:
- Memecoins
- AI tokens
- DeFi
- Gaming
- L2s

When a sector heats up:
1. Identify leaders early
2. Ride momentum
3. Exit before rotation

## Timing

### Best Trading Hours (EST)
- **9-11 AM**: US market open overlap
- **2-4 PM**: Peak US activity
- **8-10 PM**: Asia starting

### Avoid
- **3-6 AM**: Low liquidity
- **Weekends**: Unpredictable, thin books

### Event Calendar
Watch for:
- Token unlocks
- Protocol launches
- Airdrops
- Macro events (FOMC, etc.)

## Volume Analysis

### Volume Confirms Price
- Price up + Volume up = Strong trend
- Price up + Volume down = Weak, possible reversal
- Price down + Volume up = Strong selling
- Price down + Volume down = Weak selling, possible bottom

### Volume Spikes
Large volume spikes often indicate:
- Whale activity
- News event
- Trend change

## Social Sentiment

### Bullish Signals
- Increasing Twitter mentions
- Telegram/Discord activity up
- Influencers taking notice
- Reddit posts trending

### Bearish Signals
- Declining engagement
- Team going quiet
- FUD spreading
- Whales dumping publicly

## On-Chain Metrics

### Whale Watching
- Large buys = Accumulation
- Large sells = Distribution
- Wallet clustering = Coordinated activity

### DEX Activity
- Increasing swaps = Growing interest
- Liquidity additions = Confidence
- Liquidity removals = Red flag

### Holder Growth
- Steady growth = Organic
- Sudden spike = Marketing push or airdrop
- Declining = Losing interest
