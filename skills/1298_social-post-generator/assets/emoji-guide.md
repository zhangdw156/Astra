## Platform-Specific Tips

**Twitter:** 1-3 emojis max, use as bullet points
**LinkedIn:** Professional emojis only (💼📊💡), avoid excessive use
**Instagram:** Go wild! Emojis in every line work well
**Facebook:** Moderate use, 2-5 emojis per post

## Common Combinations

- Launch: 🚀🎉✨
- Tips: 💡📝👇
- Success: 🏆💪🎯
- Question: 🤔💭❓
- Link: 🔗👇📲

## Don't Use

- Too many in a row (except Instagram)
- Unclear or ambiguous emojis
- Outdated emojis
- Platform-inappropriate (e.g., 💀 on LinkedIn)
