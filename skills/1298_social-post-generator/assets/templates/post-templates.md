## Engagement Boosters

### Questions That Work
- "What's your experience with...?"
- "Agree or disagree?"
- "What would you add?"
- "Drop a 🔥 if..."
- "Tag someone who needs this"

### CTAs
- "Save this for later 📌"
- "Share with your team"
- "Comment your thoughts 👇"
- "DM me for details"
- "Link in bio"
