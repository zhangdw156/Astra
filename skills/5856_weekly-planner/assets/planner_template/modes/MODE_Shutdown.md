# MODE: Shutdown

## TRIGGER
- When: end of workday OR late evening
- Read time: 30–60s

## IDENTITY (1–2 lines)
- I am: winding down.
- Priority: protect sleep and end the day cleanly.

## RULES
- Small clean finish beats late-night heroics.
- Don’t start new work.
- Anything still on your mind gets written down.

## STEPS
1) Write down lingering thoughts / tomorrow’s first step.
2) Close the laptop.
3) Quick tidy (2–5 minutes).
4) Hygiene routine.
5) Bedtime.

## IF/THEN
- If wired or frustrated: it’s not getting fixed tonight. Park it and stop.

## DONE WHEN
- In bed, mind clear.
